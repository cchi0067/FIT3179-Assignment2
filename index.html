DENSITY OF INTERNET USERS BY COUNTRY IN 2022

Check out the Internet user density of each country!

The choropleth map below visualises the number of Internet uses per 10,000 people in different countries around the world in the year 2022. The country with the highest density of Internet users is shown to be the United Kingdom with a whopping value of 9681.17 users per 10,000 people. On the other hand, the country with the lowest density of Internet users is Somalia with a value of 196.46 users with 10,000 people.

INTERNET USERS BY CONTINENT

The global makeup of Internet users by continent.

When taking a look at Internet user makeup of each continent globally, Asia actually contributes the highest percentage to the total number of global Internet users which is 64%. Europe and North America then contribute the same percentage value of 10% to the number of global Internet users. INTERNET PLANS BY COUNTRY AND CONTINENT

The percentage of Internet plans available by country and continent.

Asia also takes the lead with the highest number of Internet plans among all continents which is 1781, making up 29% of the global number of Internet plans. Africa then follows up with 24% of the global number of Internet plans and Europe with 21%. Consistent with the Internet user percentage, the continent with the least number of Internet plans turns out to be Oceania contributing 6% to the global number.

COUNTRIES WITH FASTEST INTERNET IN 2022

Faster and faster!

Interested in knowing the top countries with the fastest Internet? Our visualisation shows that half of the countries with the fastest Internet actually hails from Asia including the country with the top Internet speed, United Arab Emirates, with an impressive speed of 135.35 Megabits per second! COUNTRIES WITH THE HIGHEST INTERNET PRICE IN 2022

What is Internet if you cannot afford it?

As with many things in life, the Internet comes with a price. In this visualisation, we take a look at the countries with the highest Internet price in terms of US dollars. It can be seen that the country with the highest Internet price is Equatorial Guinea with a price of $49.67. Another interesting observation is half of the countries that made it into the top 10 countries with the highest Internet price are from the continent of Africa.

AVERAGE INTERNET SPEED AGAINST PRICE

The more expensive, the faster?

A popular assumption is when the cost of a product is higher, it is generally better in quality. Therefore, let's take a look into whether Internet subsribers are getting their money's worth. Taking speed as a measure of Internet quality, the visualisation shows that generally, there is a positive correlation between the speed and price of Internet due to the positive coefficient of the linear regression. Hence, the hypothesis that Internet speed increases with Internet price is true.
